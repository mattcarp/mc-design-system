Design Principles

Core Guidelines

  • No Linear Gradients: Clean, solid colors throughout
  • Typography: Thin, light font weights (100-300) for elegance
  • Colors: Dark theme with blue and purple accents
  • Professional: Clean, modern, minimalist aesthetic
  • Consistency: Single source of truth for all design decisions
